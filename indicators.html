ANNOTATIONS IN NARRATIVE VISUALIZATION

This is a framework for annotation recommendations for different audiences in narrative visualization. By Jonathan Nguyen

Recommendations

1. Universal Indicator

Screenshot - 2

A Disappearing Planet

The Propublica is a website that cater towards all audiences with a senior audience (age 55-64) demographic being the largest demographic. (Similar Web)

In this example, the visualisation utilises a universal help symbol that when hovered over, produces the annotation. It always appears on the right of the symbol. Its universal symbol increases the recognition and association with requiring help and the common "?" symbol.

Recommendation

Use for a wider audience

In this example, the visualisation utilises a unconventional help symbol that when hovered over, produces the annotation. It always appears on the right of the symbol. Its unconventional symbol can help increase visual engagement and interest when apart of a consistent and unique visual scheme.
